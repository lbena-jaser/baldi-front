About Baldi Meals

We're on a mission to make healthy eating convenient, delicious, and accessible to everyone in Tunisia.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Baldi Meals was born from a simple observation: people want to eat healthy, but lack the time to prepare nutritious meals every day.

We started with a small kitchen and a big vision - to deliver restaurant-quality, nutritionally-balanced meals directly to your door.

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers across Tunisia, helping them achieve their fitness goals without sacrificing taste or convenience.
