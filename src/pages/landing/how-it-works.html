How It Works

Simple, flexible, and tailored to your goals. Get started in 3 easy steps.

1

Choose Your Plan

Select between our 5-day or 7-day meal plan based on your schedule and fitness goals. Both plans are designed by certified nutritionists.

  • 5-Day Plan: Perfect for weekday coverage
  • 7-Day Plan: Complete week coverage
  • Flexible: Pause or cancel anytime

Select Meals Image
2

Select Your Meals

Browse our weekly menu and choose from a variety of delicious, nutritionist-designed meals. Select between 5-20 meals per delivery.

  • 50+ meal options updated weekly
  • Bulking & Cutting categories available
  • Full nutritional information provided
3

Receive & Enjoy

Get fresh meals delivered to your door every week. Heat, eat, and enjoy! Your next delivery is automatically scheduled.

  • Weekly delivery on your chosen day
  • QR code verification for secure delivery
  • Next delivery auto-scheduled weekly
